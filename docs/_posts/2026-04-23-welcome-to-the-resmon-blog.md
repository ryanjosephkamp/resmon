This is the first post on the resmon blog. Going forward, every release update
to the resmon app will be mirrored here as its own post, including the full
contents of that update's `update_<DATE>.md` document, screen-recording GIFs
of new features and bug fixes (where applicable), and any other context that
helps users understand the change.

The blog is also surfaced inside the resmon app itself, on the
**About resmon → Blog** tab. The in-app reader fetches this site's feed at
`/feed.xml` and renders the post list directly from it, so any post published
here appears in the app on the next tab open without an app re-release.

Thanks for reading.
